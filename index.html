Venue

Wedding - Luray Caverns

Address: 101 Cave Hill Rd, Luray, VA 22835 - Google Maps - Apple Maps

Luray Caverns, discovered in 1878, feature a series of vast chambers adorned with towering stalactites and stalagmites, flowstone cascades, and shimmering pools that create a surreal, otherworldly environment. It is the largest and most popular cavern system in the eastern United States, renowned for its stunning underground formations and enchanting atmosphere. The wedding ceremony will take place in the Cathedral Room, offering a unique and memorable setting.

Website: https://luraycaverns.com/explore

Reception - Shawnee Farms Estate

Address: 2816 Old Forge Rd, Luray, VA 22835 - Google Maps - Apple Maps

Discover the serenity of Old Virginia at Shawnee Farms Estate. Located in the Shenandoah Valley, this historic farmstead offers views of the Shenandoah National Park and George Washington National Forest. Renovated into a wedding venue in 2020, it includes a beautiful 2 story barn with outdoor patio and separate fire pits. The estate functions as a working farm that spans 150 acres near the Shenandoah River and is minutes from downtown Luray and Luray Caverns.

Website: https://www.shawneefarmsestate.com
